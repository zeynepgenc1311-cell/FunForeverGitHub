## 1. Overview
This package contains a collection of 3D models optimized for use in Unity.  
All assets are prepared with materials for the **Built-in Render Pipeline (Default)**.  

---

## 2. Package Contents
- **Models**: FBX files  
- **Prefabs**: ready to drag & drop  
- **Materials**: Standard Shader (Built-in RP)  
- **Textures**: included where necessary  

---

## 3. Usage
- All prefabs are located in the `Prefabs` folder.  
- To use a model, simply drag the prefab into your scene.  
- Materials are already assigned to prefabs.  
- Models are correctly scaled and aligned to Unity’s coordinate system.  

---

## 4. Compatibility
- **Built-in RP (Default)**: Supported out of the box.  
- **URP / HDRP**: Materials may require manual conversion using Unity’s Render Pipeline Converter.
